  5. Michael sagt:
    21. Mai 2014 um 21:17 Uhr

    As a qualified teacher with QTS from the UK and having spend half of my life learning and teaching foreign languages, I can state that „Türkisch-kurs“ is the most enjoyable experience in my academic life. Mr. Ergün combines skillfully a didactic method in his teaching and yet provides needs of every student on an individual basis. I attended three courses (once a week) and when I flew to Turkey I was just amazed how much I could communicate with people. Mr. Erguen’s teaching is aimed as a scaffolding to assist the learners in building up their own vocabulary and understanding grammatical structures and rules in applied situations.
    Further, Mr. Ergün pays a great attention to cultural and social background of Turkish by emphasising obvious differences between Turkish spoken in Germany and Turkish spoken in Turkey. I do strongly recommend the course to students, travellers and all those who wanted to discover Turkish language and culture.
    I will definetly continue learning Turkish at the Turkish-kurs because it is a very good value for money.
